Audience

Our target audience comprises adventure-seekers and nature enthusiasts, predominantly aged 18 to 45, who are financially stable and interested in unique, outdoor activities. They are typically groups of friends, couples, or families with older children looking for exciting and safe water rafting experiences. These individuals prioritize detailed information on trip options, safety measures, and booking procedures. They mostly access our site via mobile devices for convenience. Scenario Questions and Answers: Who are the target customers? Adults and young adults, 18 to 45, interested in outdoor adventures, likely with disposable income for leisure activities. What do they want? A safe and exciting water rafting experience, with clear information on trips, preparation, and booking. What needs aren't being met? Detailed trip descriptions, safety information for different skill levels, and easy mobile access for booking and information.
